Privacy Policy – Taro Connect

Last updated: 28 December 2025

Taro Developments AUS Pty Ltd (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store and disclose personal information through the Taro Connect application and related services (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address
  • Account credentials (passwords are stored securely in encrypted form)
  • User-generated content uploaded to the App, including qualifications, documents, images, and related metadata
  • Technical data necessary to operate the App (such as device identifiers and log data)

2. How We Use Information

Information is collected and used solely to:

  • Provide and operate the Taro Connect platform
  • Authenticate users
  • Store and manage user records
  • Improve platform reliability and security
  • Communicate with users regarding their account

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is transmitted using encrypted connections. Reasonable technical and organisational safeguards are used to protect information from unauthorised access, modification or disclosure.

4. Data Ownership

All user data remains the property of the user. Taro Developments AUS Pty Ltd does not claim ownership of user content.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. Data may only be disclosed where required by law or to trusted service providers strictly for operating the platform.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

User data is retained only while the account remains active or as required by law. Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by contacting us.
